People we are looking for

If you feel underchallenged and eager to explore your limits, mesh. is where you want to be. We are looking for people who either have their own project in mind or want to try out something new. For the latter, we have plenty of ideas for you to start with. If you just want a great community to be a part of, you can also help developing it. The community itself is welcoming and open to any questions, you don’t have to be a people person, but we require that you respect the values we stand for.

What we can give you

We have an office and a workshop you can work in, meet and talk to other interesting people. If needed, we can also provide tools, material and academic help with anything. Due to our extensive network of mentors and seniors, we will be able to find a solution to all your needs. We also have a great community, in which we have dinners, beer, meaningful conversations, etc…. If you lack motivation to follow through with something, here we work by motivating each other.

What we require from a member

A member of mesh. can be active or passive. An active member is either working on a project on a weekly basis, or helping organizing events, building the community (or both). We also require that you respect the values and rules of the organizations. An inactive member can choose between being totally inactive, or “passive”. When someone is passive, other members can still ask him/her to help with an event, question or project.

What we can give and require from a senior

We organize events for them so that they can get to know each other. We also provide a platform, on which they find the best, most ambitious youngsters. Seniors can participate in nurturing the future generation of engineers. A senior is someone who contributes to and helps the community. This can be done either materially or providing knowledge with their expertise.
